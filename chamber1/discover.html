The Demographic


Income


El Progreso, Yoro, also known as Mi Cuidad Bonita (Mi pretty City), is one of the top 5 most important cities in the country. This is due the high employment being provided by the textile componies located around the city. Although, this has change in the recent days due to one of the factories closing due to the effects of the war of Russia against Ukraine.


Population


According to the Instituto Nacional de Estadistica (INE), during the year 2020, The Progreso was populated by 200,010 inhabitants.

For the social structure of the city, it's divided as follows:

  • High Class: 5%
  • Medium Class: 25%
  • Labor Class living on minimim wage or less: 50%
  • Extreme Poberty: 20%

The concentration of the population is divided in the following way:

  • Urban Area: 62%
  • Rural Area: 38%

By sex, the population is as follows:

  • Women = 105,581
  • Men = 94,430
