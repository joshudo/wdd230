The Demographic

El Progreso, Yoro, also known as mi cuidad bonita (Mi pretty City), is one of the top 5 most important cities in the country. This is due the high employment being provided by the textile componies located around the city. Although, this has change in the recent days due to one of the factories closing due to the effects of the war of Russia against Ukraine.

The density of the population is around 108,000 inhabitents. They are composed of 54,400 women, and 54,400 men. The younger population is composed of around 10800 teenagers, 10500 children between the ages of 5 - 9 years of age and 10700 babies (younger than 4 years of age). the life span average of the residents is around 24 years due to the violence the country has experience thoughout the years due to gangs and corruption.
