Visual Hierarchy

twitch.tv

twitch.tv

For Visual Hierarchy, twitch is divided in a cascading manner of importance. When entering the website one sees possible interest and then followed by categories and then by the option for exploration. This way the content is showcase by providing the most relevent elements first and the providing options to other content.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

The Apple website is a good example of this topic. They have a comprehensive design that shifts the focus and attention of the user to their products. Doing this they increase the interest of the customer by showcasing their products in a clean fashion since their brand is easy to adapt to this style.

Rule of Thirds

STAAK

STAAK

When entering the STAAK website you can appreciate the usage of the image was clearly design and capture your view by leaving unused space in certain sections of the layout. This then draws the attention to logo followed by the title and description. Your attention is naturally guided while using the rule of thirds.
