Target Audience

The target audience is to help those small and medium businesses
develop and grow by providing support through the different
resources created by the chambers.

Personas

John Doe: Someone that is wanting to start a small buisness but
doesn't know where to start and seeks the support of others to do so.

Jane Doe: Jane is wanting to know how to better promote her business
now that she has improved their production capacity.

Scenarios

  1. Representation of the voices of the buisness that make up the chamber and community.
  2. Create a comfortable space to ask any questions about business and receive the answers you need.
  3. Numerous opportunites for various people to come together and build a network of professional relationships.
  4. Providing education and experience for the communities next generation of leaders.
  5. With the inititave in reclutement, retation and infrastucture, with the goal to productive and lasting change.
