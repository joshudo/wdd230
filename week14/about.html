About Us

Bountiful Foods is a small business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, but we have food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. We sell fruits and produce directly from local farmers, but we also use some national and international suppliers to provide a variety of fresh, healthy options for our customers.

In addition to our storefront in Carlsbad, we offer made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags, as well as delivery and phone orders through our website. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic options, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.

At Bountiful Foods, we believe in the importance of healthy eating and supporting local, organic bounty. We strive to make it convenient for our customers to access fresh, healthy options, whether they are walking into our storefront or ordering from one of our food trucks.
